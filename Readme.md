[VertoXR](https://vertoxr.com/) turns compatible AR/XR glasses into a portable desktop workspace with multiple virtual displays, flexible layouts, and an immersive productivity-first experience, with support for SteamVR, WebXR, and OpenTrack.

Built for people who want to work, create, and multitask beyond the limits of a physical monitor setup, or use their glasses for a better gaming experience.

---

## Overview

VertoXR helps you extend your workspace into mixed reality so you can stay productive from anywhere.  
Instead of being tied to a single screen, you can arrange virtual displays around you and shape your setup for focus, coding, writing, designing, meetings, and more.

## What You Can Do

- Create and manage multiple virtual screens  
- Customize workspace layouts based on your workflow  
- Build a comfortable portable workstation with your AR/XR glasses  
- Move between real-world and virtual productivity seamlessly  
- Keep your setup flexible for travel, remote work, and daily multitasking  
- Play SteamVR or WebXR games with your glasses  
- Use glasses as OpenTrack head tracking device  

## Requirements

To get the full VertoXR experience, make sure you have:

1. **Compatible AR/XR glasses**  
2. **A supported desktop environment**  
3. **The latest VertoXR build**  

## Getting Started

1. Visit https://vertoxr.com/  
2. Install VertoXR on your desktop  
3. Connect your AR/XR glasses  
4. Launch and configure your workspace
